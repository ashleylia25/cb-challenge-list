# CB Challenge List
*Idea proposed by Kepler*

This website is meant for keeping track of all the Geometry Dash challenge levels created by people in CBCL.
For any questions regarding the list, DM me on Discord: meldylia25

## Includes:
1. A list of all the challenges ranked in order from hardest to easiest. (With a search bar)
2. Completions submitted by the players through Discord.
3. Stats viever - Sort of a leaderboard where players gather points and climb up the rankings with each completed challenge (the ammount of points one gains for each completion is based on the difficulty of said challenge)

## List guidelines:
### Levels:
In order for a level to be classified as a CBCL challenge, it must:
1. Be under 30 seconds,
2. Be legitimately verified,
3. Not have secret ways or LDM bypasses,
4. Be either created by or uploaded by a member of CBCL,
5. Be submitted as a challenge either by the creator or the uploader through Discord
### Players:
In order for a player to obtain list points, they must:
1. Complete levels legitimately,
2. Provide proof of completion (only footage, screenshots alone are not enough proof),
3. Submit their records through Discord,
4. Use common sense while using Mega Hack and Geode mods
